# Mandatory Invoice Information (France – 2026)

## 1. Seller Information (Your Company)
- Company name / legal name
- Full address
- SIREN / SIRET number
- VAT identification number (EU VAT number)
- Legal form (LLC, SAS, Ltd, etc.)
- Share capital (if applicable)
- Contact details (email, phone)

## 2. Buyer Information (Client)
- Client name / company name
- Full address
- EU VAT number (if EU B2B)
- Contact details (optional)

## 3. Invoice Details
- Unique invoice number
- Invoice date
- Service or delivery date (if different)
- Purchase order or quote reference (optional)

## 4. Description of Goods / Services
- Clear and precise description
- Quantity
- Unit price
- Line total
- Discounts or reductions (if any)

## 5. Mandatory Amounts
- Net amount (excluding VAT)
- VAT rate (20%, 10%, 5.5%, 2.1%, or 0%)
- VAT amount
- Total amount including VAT
- Additional fees (shipping, handling, etc.)

## 6. Mandatory VAT Legal Mentions (depending on the case)
- **Export outside EU:** "VAT not applicable – Article 259 of the French Tax Code"
- **EU B2B (reverse charge):** "Reverse charge – Article 283-2 of the French Tax Code"
- **Micro‑enterprise:** "VAT not applicable – Article 293 B of the French Tax Code"
- **Specific VAT exemption:** "VAT exemption – Article 262 of the French Tax Code"

## 7. Payment Terms
- Due date
- Payment method (bank transfer, etc.)
- Late payment penalties
- €40 fixed recovery fee (mandatory for B2B)

## 8. Additional Information
- Delivery address (if different)
- Internal order number (optional)
- Notes or additional instructions (optional)

## 9. Mandatory Data for Electronic Invoicing (Factur‑X / UBL)
- Full seller identity
- Full buyer identity
- Invoice number
- Invoice date
- Net / VAT / Total amounts
- VAT rate
- Detailed line items
- Payment terms
- VAT legal mentions

## 10. Important Reminder
You may keep a fully customized PDF invoice layout, but it must include all mandatory data listed above to be compatible with electronic invoicing (Factur‑X).
