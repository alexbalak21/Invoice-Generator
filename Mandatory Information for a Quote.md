# Mandatory Information for a Quote (Devis) — France 2026

## 1. Seller Information
- Company name / legal name
- Full address
- SIRET number
- EU VAT number
- Legal form (SAS, SARL, EI, etc.)
- RCS or RM registration number (if applicable)
- Contact details (email, phone)
- Professional insurance details (if applicable to your sector)

## 2. Client Information
- Client name or company name
- Full address
- Delivery / service address (if different)
- EU VAT number (recommended for EU B2B)

## 3. Quote Identification
- The word "QUOTE" or "DEVIS"
- Unique quote number
- Issue date
- Validity period of the offer

## 4. Description of Goods / Services
- Clear description of each item or service
- Quantity
- Unit price (excluding VAT)
- VAT rate per line
- Line total
- Total excluding VAT (HT)
- VAT amount
- Total including VAT (TTC)

## 5. Mandatory VAT Mentions
- Export outside EU: "VAT not applicable – Article 259 of the French Tax Code"
- EU B2B reverse charge: "Reverse charge – Article 283-2 of the French Tax Code"
- Micro‑enterprise: "VAT not applicable – Article 293 B of the French Tax Code"
- Specific exemption: "VAT exemption – Article 262 of the French Tax Code"

## 6. Payment Terms
- Payment method
- Due date or payment schedule
- Late payment penalties
- €40 fixed recovery fee (mandatory for B2B)

## 7. Execution / Delivery Conditions
- Estimated start date
- Estimated duration
- Specific conditions (if any)

## 8. Client Acceptance
- Acceptance mention: "Quote received before execution, read and approved, agreed."
- Client signature
- Acceptance date
